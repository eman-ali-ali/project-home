Home control is security

Have at his disposal a general control. With one single click, you can switch off all the lights and lower all the shutters when you leave home. Zero scams. Check who rings the doorbell directly from his smartphone, even in the home of elderly parents. Video surveillance. View cameras when you are away from home. For example, check if the children have returned from school.
